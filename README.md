Privacy Policy for Dora
Last Updated: January 17, 2026
Introduction
Welcome to Dora. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.
Information We Collect
Information Collected Automatically
Push Notification Data
If you enable push notifications, we collect:
A unique device identifier
Your device's push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging token for Android, APNs token for iOS)
Your device platform (Android or iOS)
This information is used solely to send you promotional updates and product announcements.
Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not require account registration or login
We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or address
We do not collect payment or financial information
We do not track your location
We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
We do not use analytics or tracking services
How We Use Your Information
The limited information we collect is used only to:
Deliver push notifications about new products, offers, and company updates (if you opt in)
Third-Party Services
Our App uses the following third-party services:
Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)
Used to deliver push notifications. Firebase may collect certain device information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
Google Maps
Used to display our store location on the Contact page. The map is displayed in view-only mode and does not track or collect your location. See Google's Privacy Policy.
Data Storage and Security
Push notification tokens are stored securely on our servers
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
Your Choices
Push Notifications
You can enable or disable push notifications at any time through:
The App's settings (in the side menu)
Your device's notification settings
When you disable notifications, we will stop sending you push notifications and mark your device as inactive in our system.
Children's Privacy
Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the App or through push notification. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
